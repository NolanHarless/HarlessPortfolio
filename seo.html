Project Scope

The following publication was created in a Web Publishing course at my University. Gillette Venus is an example of a direct to consumer (DTC) business that sells womenâ€™s razors in the form of a subscription-based service. In this publication, I conducted some traffic analysis and usability test on their online e-commerce store and then reported on those findings in a document.

What I learned

I learned how search engine ranking is determined, what content qualifies and ranks as quality content, and keyword research. I even pitted Gillette Venus against their direct competitors to see what their online store could do better in terms of usability and search engine ranking. I also learned how to conduct usability tests, interview subjects and receive input, and propose changes based on that feedback.

Most proud of

I was able to synthesize all my data and observations into one final document that I am extremely proud of. I am really proud of myself for learning all the different aspect of SEO.
